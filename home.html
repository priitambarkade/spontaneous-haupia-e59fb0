About Us

Company Overview

United International Ltd is a global trading and supply company headquartered in Mumbai, India. We specialize in sourcing and supplying a diverse range of industrial materials and products to customers across domestic and international markets.

With a strong supplier network and industry expertise, we provide reliable sourcing solutions for industries such as construction, infrastructure, manufacturing, and engineering.

Our Mission

To build long-term partnerships with customers by delivering dependable industrial supply solutions, maintaining high standards of quality, and creating value through efficient global sourcing.

Our Vision

Based in Mumbai, the commercial hub of India, United International Ltd serves customers across Asia, Middle East, Africa, and Europe. Our strategic location and strong supplier network allow us to efficiently meet the diverse requirements of international clients.
